Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a branch of computer science.
It tries to make machines think and learn like humans.
AI is used in many areas today. For example, it helps doctors with medical images,
it powers chatbots on websites, and it drives self-driving cars.
Some people are worried that AI will take jobs.
Other people think AI will create new jobs and make life easier.
The future of AI is not clear, but it is very interesting.
